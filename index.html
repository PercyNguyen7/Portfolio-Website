FASA's Lunar New Years Riso Prints

A comission by the Fine Arts Assossiation Alliance of Concordia for Lunar New Year celebration 2022!

Since the art piece is a riso print, there may only be a maximum of 4 colors. I first chose Yellow to reflect the beauty of the ochna integerrima,the yellow mai hoa mai vÃ ng in Vietnamese, a vital component of Tet (Vietnamese Lunar New Year). The second color, red, symbolizes good fortune and compliments the striking yellow. Any other colors in between the two is a mix of the two in different opacity.

The finished project was printed out and given in red envelope to celebrate with any lucky passerbys!

Medium: Photoshop

April 2022

Homelessness: The Invisible

At every metro station in Montreal lays a homeless man, sheltering away from the heat of summer or the winter cold. I researched and created a short, informative website .tackling the statistics of homelessness in Canada as well as the causes behind them. It also offers a unique experience that imitates a fraction of the sufferings that homeless people go through daily.

Tip: With your mouse, navigate through the end of the "Experience" page carefully to reveal hidden clue.

Currently available in desktop view. Will be responsive in the future if time permits.

Medium: Javascript, CSS, HTML, Adobe Illustrator.
